Content design

Our content standards are for anyone creating content for the MHLCG. The standards help us to make things clear and consistent across all of our digital services.

We are currently working on defining and publishing the standards within our user centred design community. The standards will include:

  • A to Z style guide
  • tone of voice
  • MHCLG glossary
  • how to write emails
  • types of content we publish
  • how to get content reviewed
  • content strategy

Our standards are designed to build on and compliment the existing GDS content standards. We will continue to refer to those standards when designing our digital services.
